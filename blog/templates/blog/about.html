Our Family

We are a happy family of four! Momma (Adrienne), Poppa (Alex), Sweetheart (Ava), and Choppie (Chopper). One baby girl and one furry four legged baby!

After we had our daughter, we started compiling a list of all the items that we could NOT live without for our friends who were soon to be expecting. We thought that other people outside of our circle of friends would want to know what worked for us too--so we started this blog!

Our Goal

We want to provide you with all of the items that we think will make your baby the happiest! These items were used on our sweetheart and we would not be recommending them to you otherwise!
